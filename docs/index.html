02.5 · Stakeholder salience

Mitchell–Agle–Wood.
Every voice weighted.

Power × Legitimacy × Urgency determines each stakeholder group's weight in the materiality aggregation. Only stakeholders with all three attributes reach Definitive status.

Definitive · Weight 12% each
Regulators Customers Shareholders Employees

All three MAW attributes present (Power + Legitimacy + Urgency). These groups can demand action — and expect compliance — simultaneously.

Expectant · Weight 8% each
Investors Suppliers Partners Society Communities Environment

Two attributes (Dominant: P+L, or Dependent: L+U). Actively engaged but lack the full combination to compel unilateral action.

Latent · Weight 4% (Discretionary)
Academia

Legitimacy attribute only. Influential on long horizons via research partnerships; limited power and urgency in water-utility operations.
