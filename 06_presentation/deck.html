03 · How we tested the case

We did not assert.
We modelled.

Impact Severity  =  mean(Scale, Scope, Remediability) × Probability
Financial Severity  =  Magnitude × Probability × Horizon discount
Aqualia's own nomenclature preserved, so results are directly comparable. Terminology mapping to the Datathon brief §3: Scale ✓ · Scope ✓ · Likelihood → Probability · Irreversibility → Remediability.
(i)
Thresholds cited
Every 1–5 score band anchors to ESRS 1 §43–51, EFRAG IG-1, SASB Water Utilities, WRI Aqueduct, or MSCI 2024. No authorial opinion.
(ii)
Stakeholder salience — Mitchell–Agle–Wood (1997)
The standard academic model for ranking stakeholders by how much their claims matter. Four reach Definitive in Aqualia's ecosystem: Regulators, Customers, Shareholders, Employees.
(iii)
Monte Carlo uncertainty
10,000 draws per topic, plausible variation on every input. The matrix shows confidence ellipses — where each topic could land — not false-precision points.
(iv)
ESRS gap heatmap
Bilingual TF-IDF across 873 paragraph chunks of Aqualia's 2022–2025 reports, scoring 75 canonical ESRS datapoints for coverage.
